================================================================================
    PAYMENT FLOW OVERVIEW
================================================================================

[1] Diver Creates Booking
    ↓
[2] Diver Requests Payment
    ↓
[3] System Generates QR Code + Link
    ├─ QR code (scannable)
    ├─ Payment link (shareable)
    └─ Expires in 5 minutes
    ↓
[4] Diver Scans QR or Clicks Link
    ├─ Opens Bit app
    ├─ Confirms amount
    ├─ Authenticates
    └─ Sends payment
    ↓
[5] Bit System Processes Payment (Instant)
    ↓
[6] Backend Verifies Payment
    ├─ Updates booking → "confirmed"
    ├─ Records transaction
    ├─ Calculates commission (8%)
    └─ Creates payout record
    ↓
[7] Settlement Next Business Day (T+1)
    ├─ Provider receives net amount
    └─ DIVE DROP receives commission

COMMISSION EXAMPLE:
Booking: 500 (50000 cents)
Commission (8%): 40 (4000 cents)
Provider Payout: 460 (46000 cents)
