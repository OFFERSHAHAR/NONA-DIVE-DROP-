WHAT WAS BUILT
================================================================================

A complete depth-based training matching system for free diving that:

1. CATEGORIZES TRAINING BY DEPTH LEVEL
   - Beginner (0-10m)
   - Intermediate (10-25m)
   - Advanced (25-40m)
   - Expert (40m+)

2. INTELLIGENTLY MATCHES USERS WITH TRAINING
   - Analyzes 5 factors: depth, experience, location, price, quality
   - Generates confidence score (0-100%)
   - Prevents booking above current level
   - Provides personalized recommendations

3. TRACKS USER PROGRESSION
   - Records depth achieved
   - Manages certifications
   - Tracks medical clearances
   - Monitors emergency contact info

4. MANAGES ENROLLMENTS
   - Full enrollment workflow
   - Completion tracking
   - Feedback collection
   - Certification issuance
